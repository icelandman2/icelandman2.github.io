Travel (UNDER CONSTRUCTION)

My sophomore year at Stanford, I met some folks who taught me how to travel for far less money than I thought possible. I later went on to become a founding member of Traveling Trees, the first VSO dedicated to travel hacking. Last year, I flew 122,831 miles all over the world, and currently hold Alaska Airlines MVP Gold 75K status. The vast majority of my trips are with my girlfriend, Jackie. Enjoy!
