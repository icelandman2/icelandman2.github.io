Pricing and Quotes

Exact pricing varies with the nature and complexity of your request. My average rate is $335 per hour, though may be higher or lower depending on the details of the project. Volume discounts are available. Pro Bono work is available on behalf of 501(c)(3) organizations!

Lead Time

Due to the requirement that I complete an employer disclosure prior to starting any outside work, for best results please contact me at least 2-3 weeks prior to the planned start date of any consulting engagement.

Employer Approval and No-Go Zones

Due to the nature of my work, for regulatory reasons I am required to disclose to my primary employer if I am completing work for outside entities. For the same reason, I am generally unable to assist on projects that involve capital markets or the financial services industry.
