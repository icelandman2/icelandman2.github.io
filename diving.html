Diving

My dad learned to dive in 1969, when diving was still in its infancy. Right after I graduated from high school, he took me on an introductory dive while our family vacationed in Maui, and I was immediately hooked. I got my Open Water certification in Fall 2015 and my AOW in Spring 2019, while diving whenever circumstances (and my wallet) allowed along the way. This section shows some photos from my diving adventures - unfortunately, I did not bring a camera on many of my dives, including off Monterey when I saw a Great White this past May!
