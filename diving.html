Diving (UNDER CONSTRUCTION)

My dad learned to dive in 1969, when diving was still in its infancy. Right after I graduated from high school, he took me on an introductory dive while our family vacationed in Maui, and I was immediately hooked. I got my Open Water certification in Fall 2015 and my AOW in Spring 2019, while diving whenever circumstances (and my wallet) allowed along the way. When complete, this section will showcase some of the locales annd creatures I have seen while diving! For now, the photos will just be duplicated from those shown in the travel section.
